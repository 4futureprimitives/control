Waves

Henri Lefebreve pointed to "waves" as the uncertain momentum of modernity, a legacy pulsing through so many struggles. Resistance moves like backwash and feedback against convention in art, music and film, resounding into the world. From an indigenous perspective, the wave moves in cycles along nodal connections between past, present and future. From data visualization to computer music, the wave is a contant movement of numbers that aid and repudiate an authoritarian desire for projection, representation, and replication.
